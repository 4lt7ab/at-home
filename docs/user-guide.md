# at-home User Guide

A simple notes app for capturing and organizing your thoughts, lists, and reference information. Real-time updates keep everything in sync across tabs and devices.

---

## Getting Started

When you open the app, you land on the **Notes** page. This is the only page -- a clean list of all your notes.

The top bar has a theme toggle button and a small colored dot. The dot is green when the app has a live connection to the server; red means it is disconnected.

---

## Working with Notes

Notes have two fields: a **title** (required) and **context** (optional freeform text). Use them for anything -- reminders, reference info, lists, meeting notes, ideas.

### Creating a Note

1. Click the **+ New Note** button.
2. Enter a title and optional context in the overlay form.
3. Click **Create**.

The new note appears in the list immediately.

### Editing a Note

Click on a note to open it. Edit the title or context, then save your changes.

### Deleting a Note

Open a note and use the delete action to remove it permanently.

---

## UI Features

### Dark Mode

The theme toggle button in the top bar cycles through three modes:

- **Auto** (half-circle icon) -- follows your operating system's light/dark preference.
- **Light** (sun icon) -- forces light mode regardless of OS setting.
- **Dark** (moon icon) -- forces dark mode regardless of OS setting.

Your choice is remembered between sessions.

### Real-Time Updates

The green dot in the top bar indicates a live connection to the server. When connected, any changes -- whether made in another browser tab, through the API, or via an AI assistant -- appear automatically without refreshing the page. If the dot turns red, the app will attempt to reconnect automatically.
